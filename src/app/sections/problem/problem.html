The Problem

The systems running your business aren't going anywhere.

A typical enterprise is held together by more than a decade of layered technology — ERPs, mainframes, data warehouses, a sprawl of SaaS tools, and a Confluence space nobody has opened since onboarding.

Most AI tooling assumes all of that already sits in one tidy repository. Cortex doesn't. It runs between the systems you already have and the models you want to use, so your assistants, agents, and copilots can read your real data — without anyone moving it.
