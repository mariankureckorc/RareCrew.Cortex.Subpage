The Platform

Four building blocks between your data and your models.

Universal connectors

Ready-made adapters for 80+ enterprise systems including SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, and ServiceNow, plus any SQL, REST, or GraphQL source. Connect in place — no custom pipelines to build.

Row-level permissions

Cortex reads from the access rules you already run. If someone can't see a record in SAP today, neither can their assistant. There's no second permissions model to maintain.

Model routing

Send each request to Claude, GPT, Gemini, or a private model based on cost, speed, or sensitivity — and switch providers anytime without rewriting a single downstream app.

Governance & audit

Every prompt, retrieval, and answer is recorded with who asked, what was cited, and what it cost — an audit trail your compliance team can hand to regulators from day one.
